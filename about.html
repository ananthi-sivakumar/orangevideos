Who We Are

Bringing Ideas to Life Through Video

At Orange Videos, we're more than a production company — we're storytellers and creators. Founded in Mumbai with a passion for visual storytelling, we've spent 13+ years crafting videos that connect, inspire, and drive real business results.

From corporate films to brand videos, explainers, animations, and ad films — we produce every format with the same obsessive attention to quality. Our in-house team of 70+ creatives, combined with a global network of 100+ professionals, ensures every project is a masterpiece.

With offices in Mumbai, Bangalore, and the UAE, we serve clients across 12 countries and every major industry — from BFSI and healthcare to fashion and FMCG.

Our Journey

13 Years of Excellence

2011
Founded in Mumbai
Orange Videos was born from a simple belief — that every brand deserves a compelling visual story. Started with a small crew and a big vision in Andheri East, Mumbai.
2014
First 100 Projects
Crossed the 100-project milestone with clients spanning manufacturing, healthcare, and FMCG. Our reputation for on-time, high-quality delivery was established.
2017
International Expansion
Served our first international clients, delivering video content for brands across the Middle East and Southeast Asia. UAE operations commenced.
2020
Digital-First Pivot
Expanded into digital ad films, social media videos, and explainer animations. 2D & 3D animation studio fully operational with a dedicated in-house team.
2022
Bangalore Studio Opens
Third studio launched in Bangalore's Indiranagar. Team grew to 70+ creatives serving brands across South India and globally.
2024
AI Video Production Launch
Pioneered AI video production services in India, combining generative AI with cinematic storytelling for next-gen brand content.
