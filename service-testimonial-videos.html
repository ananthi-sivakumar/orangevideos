Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

+How do you get customers to agree to be filmed?+
We provide a professional briefing process that explains the low time commitment (typically 2 hours on location) and the professional production quality, which most customers find flattering rather than burdensome.
+How long should a testimonial video be?+
2–3 minutes is ideal for the main film. We also produce 45–60 second cut-downs for ads and social media use.
+Can you shoot in our clients' offices across different cities?+
Yes — we have production crews in Mumbai, Bangalore, Delhi, and Hyderabad, and we regularly coordinate pan-India shoots.
