Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

+How long does a typical video project take?+
Timelines vary by complexity. A corporate video typically takes 3–4 weeks, an ad film 4–6 weeks, and an animated explainer 4–8 weeks from brief approval to final delivery.
+Do you work with clients outside of India?+
Absolutely. We have served clients across 12 countries including the UAE, USA, UK, and Southeast Asia. Our UAE studio handles Middle East productions locally.
+What is your minimum project budget?+
We work across a range of budgets. For ad films and brand films, projects typically start at ₹1.5L. Explainers and animated videos can start from ₹75K depending on duration and complexity.
+Do you handle scripting and creative direction?+
Yes — we provide end-to-end production services including strategy, scripting, storyboarding, casting, direction, cinematography, and post-production. You can come to us with just an idea.
+How many revisions are included?+
All projects include structured revision rounds — typically 2 rounds of feedback at script stage and 2 rounds at edit stage. Additional revisions are accommodated as needed.
+Can you shoot across multiple cities in India?+
Yes. With studios in Mumbai and Bangalore and crew networks across India, we can produce content in any city. We regularly shoot in Delhi, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, and beyond.
