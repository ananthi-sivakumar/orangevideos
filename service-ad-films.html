How We Work

Our Ad Films Production Process

01
Brief & Discovery
We deep-dive into your brand, product, target audience, campaign goals, and competitive landscape before touching the script.
02
Script & Storyboard
Our creative team develops multiple script concepts. The approved script gets a detailed storyboard for visual alignment before production.
03
Pre-Production
Location scouting, casting, art direction, crew planning, shot lists — every production detail is locked before the camera rolls.
04
Production / Shoot
Our directors and DoPs bring the storyboard to life with cinematic lighting, composition, and performance direction.
05
Post-Production
Edit, color grading, sound design, VFX, and music — we craft the final film to the highest technical and creative standards.
06
Delivery
We deliver final files in all required formats and resolutions for TV broadcast, digital platforms, and social media.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

+How long does it take to produce an ad film?+
A typical ad film takes 4–6 weeks from brief to delivery — including scripting, pre-production, a 1–2 day shoot, and post-production. Timelines are flexible based on complexity.
+What is the minimum budget for an ad film?+
Ad film projects at Orange Videos typically start from ₹1.5L and scale up based on production scope, locations, cast, and VFX requirements.
+Do you write the script?+
Yes — our creative team handles scripting, conceptualization, and storyboarding as part of our end-to-end service. We can also work from your existing creative brief.
+Can you shoot across multiple locations?+
Absolutely. We regularly shoot across Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, and international locations including the UAE.
